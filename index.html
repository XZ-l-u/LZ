About Lynette Zeng

I help public organizations and nonprofits strengthen financial accuracy, compliance, and decision-making through practical analytics and clear reporting.

With a background spanning accounting, business analytics, information systems, and public-sector operations, I work at the intersection of finance and data—where accuracy matters and trust is essential.

I have supported organizations where leadership depends on reliable information to make responsible decisions. Through that experience, I've seen how unclear data, weak controls, or rushed reporting can undermine confidence and create unnecessary risk.

My approach is calm, independent, and practical. I focus on:

  • Improving financial clarity
  • Strengthening internal controls
  • Translating complex data into decision-ready insights

I believe good governance starts with trustworthy data. My work helps organizations move from uncertainty to confidence—without unnecessary complexity.
